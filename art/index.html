Currently Obsessed With

The unnamed oil painting that changed everything

Some pieces you walk past. This one stopped me mid-aisle at an estate sale on a Wednesday morning. No signature, no label, no frame — just the painting, leaning against a filing cabinet. I paid $6. It's now the first thing I see every morning.

That's what collecting really is: the willingness to stop and look before someone tells you whether you're supposed to.
