About

Buy less.
Find better.

Mott Spaces is a personal archive of thrift finds, vintage objects, and secondhand discoveries. Every piece here was found in a bin, on a shelf, or at a sale — never bought new.

The point isn't frugality. It's that pre-owned objects carry something new things never will: a history you get to continue.
